## 🍅 The Pomodoro Technique

### What is Pomodoro?
The Pomodoro Technique breaks work into focused 25-minute intervals followed by short breaks. It's scientifically proven to:
- **Improve concentration** and reduce mental fatigue
- **Increase productivity** by creating urgency
- **Prevent burnout** with regular breaks
- **Build sustainable work habits**

### How to Use Pomodoro in Academic Buddy

1. **Select Pomodoro Mode** on the Focus page
2. **Choose your task** (optional but recommended)
3. **Add a tag** to categorize your work
4. **Click START** and focus for 25 minutes
5. **Take your break** when the timer rings
6. **Repeat the cycle** - every 4 cycles gets a longer break!

### 🎛️ Customizing Your Pomodoro
- **Focus Duration**: Default 25 minutes (customize in settings)
- **Short Break**: Default 5 minutes
- **Long Break**: Default 15 minutes (after 4 cycles)
- **Auto-start**: Choose if breaks start automatically

## ⏱️ Stopwatch Mode

### When to Use Stopwatch
Perfect for tasks that don't fit the 25-minute Pomodoro mold:
- **Creative work** that needs flow state
- **Reading sessions** of varying lengths
- **Problem-solving** that requires deep thinking
- **Flexible study sessions**

### Stopwatch Features
- **No time limits** - work as long as you need
- **Maximum time alerts** - set optional time caps
- **Pause and resume** - take breaks when needed
- **Session tracking** - all time is recorded for analytics

## 🎯 Maximizing Your Focus Sessions

### 🔥 Before You Start
- **Clear your workspace** of distractions
- **Turn off notifications** on your devices
- **Have water and snacks** ready
- **Set a clear intention** for what you'll accomplish

### 💪 During Your Session
- **Single-task focus** - resist the urge to multitask
- **Use the fullscreen mode** for minimal distractions
- **Keep a notepad** for capturing random thoughts
- **Trust the timer** - don't check the clock constantly

### 🎉 After Your Session
- **Rate your focus quality** honestly (1-10 scale)
- **Add session notes** about what you accomplished
- **Take your break seriously** - step away from work
- **Celebrate small wins** - every session counts!

## 🚀 Advanced Focus Techniques

### The 2-Minute Rule
If a task takes less than 2 minutes, do it immediately instead of adding it to your focus session.

### Time Blocking
- **Schedule focus sessions** in advance
- **Batch similar tasks** together
- **Protect your focus time** like important meetings
- **Plan breaks** as intentionally as work time

### Energy Management
- **Match task difficulty** to your energy levels
- **Use high-energy times** for challenging work
- **Save routine tasks** for low-energy periods
- **Take real breaks** - no work-related activities

### Flow State Triggers
- **Clear goals**: Know exactly what you're working on
- **Immediate feedback**: Use progress indicators
- **Challenge-skill balance**: Tasks should be appropriately difficult
- **Deep concentration**: Eliminate all distractions

## 🎯 Common Focus Session Scenarios

### 📚 **Study Session**
1. Choose **Pomodoro mode** for structured learning
2. Tag with **subject name** (Math, History, etc.)
3. Focus on **one topic** per session
4. Use breaks for **quick review** or **mental rest**

### 💻 **Coding/Writing**
1. Use **Stopwatch mode** for flow state work
2. Set a **maximum time** to prevent overwork
3. Tag with **project name** or **activity type**
4. Take breaks to **prevent eye strain**

### 📖 **Reading & Research**
1. **Pomodoro** works great for dense material
2. **Stopwatch** for lighter reading or when in flow
3. Tag with **source** or **research topic**
4. Use session notes to **capture key insights**

### 🧠 **Problem Solving**
1. Start with **Pomodoro** for structured thinking
2. Switch to **Stopwatch** if you hit a breakthrough
3. Use breaks for **subconscious processing**
4. Rate sessions based on **progress made**
