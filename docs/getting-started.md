# 🚀 Getting Started with Academic Buddy

Welcome to Academic Buddy! Let's get you set up and productive in just a few minutes.

## 🎯 What is Academic Buddy?

Academic Buddy is your all-in-one productivity companion that combines:
- **🍅 Focus Sessions**: Pomodoro timer and stopwatch for deep work
- **📊 Smart Analytics**: Comprehensive insights into your productivity patterns
- **📋 Project Management**: Notion-style organization with Kanban boards
- **🎓 Study Tools**: Everything you need to excel academically

---

## 🏁 Quick Start (5 Minutes)

### Step 1: Create Your Account
1. Visit the Academic Buddy homepage
2. Click **"Get Started"** or **"Sign Up"**
3. Choose your preferred method:
   - 📧 **Email & Password**: Create a custom account
   - 🔍 **Google Sign-In**: Quick setup with your Google account

### Step 2: Your First Look Around
After signing in, you'll land on the **Focus page** - your productivity command center!

**🧭 Navigation Sidebar** (left side):
- **⏰ Focus**: Your main workspace for focus sessions
- **📊 Analytics**: Detailed insights and productivity trends  
- **📋 Projects**: Organize your work with Kanban boards

### Step 3: Start Your First Focus Session
1. **Choose your timer mode**:
   - 🍅 **Pomodoro**: 25-minute focused work + 5-minute breaks
   - ⏱️ **Stopwatch**: Flexible timing for any task

2. **Optional setup**:
   - Select a task to work on
   - Add tags for better organization
   - Adjust timer settings

3. **Hit the big START button** and begin focusing! 🎯

### Step 4: Complete Your Session
- Work until the timer ends
- Rate your focus quality (1-10)
- Add notes about what you accomplished
- Save your session to track progress

## 🎨 Customizing Your Experience

### 🔊 Sound & Notifications
- **Toggle sound effects** for session start/end

### 🎯 Tags & Organization

- **Track time** spent on different activities

### ⚙️ Timer Settings
- **Customize Pomodoro intervals** (focus/break durations)
- **Set maximum stopwatch time** for time-boxed work
- **Choose your preferred work rhythm**
