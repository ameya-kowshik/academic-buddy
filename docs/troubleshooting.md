# 🔧 Troubleshooting Guide

Quick solutions to common issues and frequently asked questions about Academic Buddy.

## 🚨 Common Issues & Quick Fixes

### 🔊 Audio Problems

**Problem**: No sound when timer starts/ends
**Solutions**:
- ✅ Check if sound is enabled in focus page settings
- ✅ Verify browser volume is not muted
- ✅ Try refreshing the page
- ✅ Check system volume settings
- ✅ Test with different browser if issue persists

**Problem**: Sound is too loud/quiet
**Solutions**:
- ✅ Adjust volume in browser tab
- ✅ Use system volume controls
- ✅ Check if browser has per-site volume settings

### 📱 Notification Issues

**Problem**: Not receiving break reminders
**Solutions**:
- ✅ Allow notifications when browser prompts
- ✅ Check browser notification settings
- ✅ Ensure Academic Buddy tab stays open
- ✅ Verify system notification settings
- ✅ Try enabling notifications in browser settings

**Problem**: Too many notifications
**Solutions**:
- ✅ Disable notifications in focus page settings
- ✅ Adjust browser notification preferences
- ✅ Use "Do Not Disturb" mode during focus sessions

### ⏰ Timer Problems

**Problem**: Timer doesn't start
**Solutions**:
- ✅ Refresh the page and try again
- ✅ Check internet connection
- ✅ Clear browser cache and cookies
- ✅ Try a different browser
- ✅ Disable browser extensions temporarily

**Problem**: Timer stops unexpectedly
**Solutions**:
- ✅ Keep the Academic Buddy tab active
- ✅ Avoid putting computer to sleep
- ✅ Check for browser updates
- ✅ Ensure stable internet connection

### 💾 Data & Sync Issues

**Problem**: Sessions not saving
**Solutions**:
- ✅ Check internet connection
- ✅ Verify you're logged in
- ✅ Try logging out and back in
- ✅ Clear browser cache
- ✅ Check if browser is blocking cookies

**Problem**: Analytics not updating
**Solutions**:
- ✅ Refresh the analytics page
- ✅ Wait a few minutes for data to sync
- ✅ Check if sessions are saving properly
- ✅ Try logging out and back in

### 🚪 Can't Log In

**Problem**: Forgot password
**Solutions**:
- ✅ Use "Forgot Password" link on login page
- ✅ Check email (including spam folder)
- ✅ Try password reset multiple times if needed
- ✅ Contact support if email doesn't arrive

**Problem**: Google Sign-In not working
**Solutions**:
- ✅ Clear browser cookies for Google
- ✅ Try incognito/private browsing mode
- ✅ Disable ad blockers temporarily
- ✅ Check if third-party cookies are enabled

**Problem**: Account locked or suspended

- ✅ Use account recovery options

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### 🎯 General Usage

**Q: How long should my focus sessions be?**
A: Start with 25 minutes (Pomodoro) and adjust based on your attention span and task complexity.

**Q: Can I pause a focus session?**
A: Stopwatch mode allows pausing. Pomodoro mode is designed to run continuously for best results.

**Q: How do I delete old sessions?**
A: Currently, sessions cannot be deleted individually. This helps maintain accurate analytics.

**Q: Can I use Academic Buddy offline?**
A: No, Academic Buddy requires internet connection for saving data and syncing across devices.

### 📊 Analytics Questions

**Q: Why don't I see today's data in analytics?**
A: Complete at least one focus session, then wait a few minutes for data to sync.

**Q: How is focus score calculated?**
A: Focus score is your self-rating (1-10) of how well you concentrated during the session.

**Q: What counts as a "streak day"?**
A: Any day with at least one completed focus session counts toward your streak.

**Q: Can I export my analytics data?**
A: Data export features are planned for future updates.

### 🔐 Account Questions

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Yes, update your email in account settings. You may need to verify the new address.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact support for account deletion. This action cannot be undone.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, all data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share personal information.
