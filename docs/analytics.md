## 🎯 Key Metrics Explained

### 📊 Focus Score (1-10)
**What it means**: Your self-rated quality of focus during a session
- **8-10**: Excellent focus, deep work achieved
- **6-7**: Good focus with minor distractions
- **4-5**: Average focus, some wandering attention
- **1-3**: Poor focus, many distractions

**How to improve**: Eliminate distractions, choose appropriate task difficulty, work during peak energy times

### 🔥 Streaks
**Current Streak**: Consecutive days with at least one focus session
**Best Streak**: Your personal record for consecutive days

**Why streaks matter**: Consistency beats intensity. A 30-day streak of 25-minute sessions (12.5 hours) often produces better results than sporadic 4-hour marathon sessions.

### ⏱️ Session Length
**Average Session Length**: How long your typical focus session lasts
**Total Focus Time**: Cumulative time spent in focused work

**Optimization tip**: Aim for consistency rather than duration. Regular 25-minute sessions often outperform irregular long sessions.

### 🏷️ Tag Distribution
**What it shows**: How you allocate time across different subjects or activities
**Why it's useful**: Ensures balanced attention to all important areas

## 🔍 Reading Your Data

### 📈 Positive Trends to Look For
- **Increasing daily averages** over time
- **Consistent streaks** building up
- **Higher focus scores** as you improve
- **Balanced tag distribution** across priorities

### ⚠️ Warning Signs to Address
- **Declining focus scores** - may indicate burnout
- **Inconsistent patterns** - need better scheduling
- **Imbalanced tag distribution** - neglecting important areas
- **Very short sessions** - may need better environment

### 🎯 Goal Setting with Analytics
- **Use your best streak** as a target to beat
- **Set daily minimums** based on your averages
- **Balance tag time** according to your priorities
- **Improve focus scores** through environment optimization

## 💡 Analytics-Driven Productivity Tips

### 🕐 Find Your Peak Times
1. **Review your daily timeline** for several weeks
2. **Identify when you have highest focus scores**
3. **Schedule your most important work** during these windows
4. **Protect these times** from meetings and distractions

### 📊 Balance Your Focus Areas
1. **Check your tag distribution** weekly
2. **Ensure important subjects** get adequate time
3. **Adjust your schedule** if any area is neglected
4. **Use colors** to quickly spot imbalances

### 🎯 Set Data-Driven Goals
- **Streak goals**: "I'll maintain a 30-day streak"
- **Time goals**: "I'll focus for 2 hours daily"
- **Quality goals**: "I'll achieve 8+ focus scores"
- **Balance goals**: "I'll spend equal time on all subjects"

### 📈 Track Improvement Over Time
- **Compare months** to see long-term trends
- **Celebrate improvements** in any metric
- **Adjust strategies** based on what the data shows
- **Share progress** with study partners for accountability
